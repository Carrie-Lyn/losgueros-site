Ride With Us

Rueda con nosotros

Interested in joining our motorcycle club? Women and men of every background are welcome. There are two types of membership, and both come with full voting rights.

¿Te interesa unirte a nuestro club de motociclismo? Mujeres y hombres de todo origen son bienvenidos. Hay dos tipos de membresía, y ambas incluyen derecho a voto completo.

🏍️

Full (Riding) Member

Miembro Pleno (Rodador)

For riders who own a motorcycle in México and ride with the club on a regular basis.

Para quienes tienen una motocicleta en México y ruedan con el club de forma regular.

★ Full voting rights · Voto completo
🤝

Associate (Non-Riding) Member

Miembro Asociado (No Rodador)

For anyone who doesn't own a motorcycle in México and/or won't be riding with the club regularly.

Para quienes no tienen una motocicleta en México y/o no rodarán con el club de forma regular.

★ Full voting rights · Voto completo
